Created outbound-caller.js with multiple call modes using your Twilio conference number (+16073094981).

  Key Features:
  - 4 Call Modes: Test call, conference join, AI bot call, custom TwiML
  - Easy Configuration: Update TARGET_PHONE and SELECTED_MODE at the top
  - Integrates with Your Systems: Uses your conference name "Zeus_Conference" and ngrok URLs
  - Safety Features: 3-second countdown, clear instructions, error handling

  To Use:
  1. Update TARGET_PHONE with the number you want to call
  2. Choose your SELECTED_MODE (currently set to TEST_CALL)
  3. Run: node outbound-caller.js

  The script will automatically use your Twilio credentials from .env and your conference number as the caller      
  ID.
